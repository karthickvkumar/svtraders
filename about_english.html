About US

                  S.V Traders, rice manufacturers, exporters and suppliers in Tamil Nadu. Established in the year 2000 by Mr. K.Ponraj and Mr. K.Raji. They started rice mill in small level and by their undeterred effort the production capacity came to 1000 Kgs per day. Today the combined efforts of together has taken the S.V Traders to greater heights of 10 Tons Production Capacity Per Day.

                  Our Rice varieties includes Boiled rice, Rajabogam rice, Thanjavur Ponni rice, Deluxe Ponni rice, Steamed rice. Manufactured rice are well cleaned according to high standards of quality, hygienically processed and packed through latest state of art, imported machines to maintain highest international standards. Our brand rice are exported from Tamil Nadu to various states.We are equally committed to enhancing our capacities and introducing the latest milling techniques to the rice industry.
